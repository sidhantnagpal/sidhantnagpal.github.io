about

Hi, I am Sidhant Nagpal. Welcome to my blog.
I am a Software Engineer at Tower Research Capital. I like to work on low-latency systems, algorithms, machine learning. Previously, I was a Google Summer of Code (GSoC) student developer and mentor for SymPy. In the past, I have participated in ACM-ICPC Asia regionals and Google Code Jam semi-finals. I like doing competitive programming and contributing to open source. Details of my contributions can be found on GitHub. I completed my Bachelor's in Computer Science Engineering from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology, University of Delhi in 2019. My bachelor thesis involved research on Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs) and it has been included in IEEE conference proceedings of IJCNN 2020.
